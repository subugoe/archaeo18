At first the legs were wanting; but they were found in 1560, & came into the possession of Prince Borghese,
who refused for some time to give them up. They are now however rejoined to the body. In the mean time a fresh
pair of legs had been executed by Gulielmo della Porta, under the direction of Michael Angelo, & these may now be seen
in the Farnese Palace at Rome.
